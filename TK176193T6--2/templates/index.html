Towards a Secure Electronic Health Record System Using Blockchain Technology.

The title "Towards a Secure Electronic Health Record System Using Blockchain Technology" focuses on leveraging cutting-edge technologies to address two critical challenges in modern healthcare: data security and disease prediction. The use of Elliptic Curve Cryptography (ECC) ensures robust encryption, securing sensitive patient data across distributed systems, particularly in decentralized healthcare environments like telemedicine or cloud-based platforms. Concurrently, AI-driven disease prediction models, enhanced through Transfer Learning, accelerate the development of accurate diagnostic systems by utilizing pre-trained models, which reduce the need for extensive data. This integration of ECC for data protection and Transfer Learning for predictive accuracy can transform healthcare by offering secure, efficient, and precise diagnostic solutions, benefiting both patients and providers in distributed healthcare networks.
